Something About Me

Hello! Hello, my name is Haowei Wu. I'm from Guangdong, China, and I'm I am majoring in Communication Design at CityTech. I enjoy design because it is a field that allows you to be creative and express your ideas and emotions through elements such as graphics, colors, and shapes. I am also trying to express myself through my own efforts. A good design can resonate, leave a lasting impression, and even change people's perceptions and behaviors. Design is not only about aesthetics, but also about the utility and functionality of a product, space or interface. Through design, it is possible to create something that is both aesthetically pleasing and functional. I hope to learn to create works that will leave an impact on the world.
